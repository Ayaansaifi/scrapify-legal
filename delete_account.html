🗑️ Delete Your Account

We're sorry to see you go. Here's how you can permanently delete your Scrapify account and all associated data.

How to Delete Your Account

  1. Open the Scrapify app on your device
  2. Go to Profile tab (bottom right)
  3. Tap on Settings (⚙️ icon)
  4. Scroll down to Danger Zone
  5. Tap Delete Account
  6. Confirm your decision when prompted

What Gets Deleted

  • ✓ Your profile information (name, email, phone)
  • ✓ Your address and location history
  • ✓ All chat messages
  • ✓ Order/pickup history
  • ✓ Uploaded photos
  • ✓ Reviews and ratings you've given
⚠️ Warning: Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. All your data will be erased within 30 days of deletion request.
