Delete Your Scrapify Account

Package: com.kabadiwala.app. Use these steps to permanently delete your account and associated personal data (Google Play requirement).

How to delete in the app

  1. Open the Scrapify app
  2. Go to the Profile tab
  3. Open Settings
  4. Scroll to Danger Zone
  5. Tap Delete Account
  6. Confirm when prompted (you may see two confirmations)

What we delete

  • Your profile (name, email, phone, city, photo reference)
  • Your marketplace listings and listing photos we can reach
  • Your status/stories and scrap rates
  • Your favorites, blocks/reports you created
  • Chat rooms/messages linked to your account where possible
  • Your Appwrite authentication account (login)
Warning: Deletion is permanent. Some backups or legal/safety records may take up to 30 days to fully clear. Messages other people sent may remain in their history.

If you cannot open the app

Email support@kabadiwala.app from your registered email, or WhatsApp +91 8586040076. Include your registered email or phone number.
