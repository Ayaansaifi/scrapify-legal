🛡️ Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2026

1. Information We Collect

Scrapify ("we", "our", "us") collects the following information to provide our scrap pickup and recycling services:

  • Account Information: Name, email, phone number when you register.
  • Location Data: Your device location to show nearby scrap dealers and calculate pickup routes.
  • Photos: Images you upload of scrap items for price estimation.
  • Chat Messages: Communications with dealers through our in-app chat.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your data to:

  • Connect you with verified scrap dealers
  • Provide accurate scrap price estimates
  • Facilitate pickups and payments
  • Improve our services and user experience
  • Send important service notifications

3. Data Sharing

We share your information only with:

  • Scrap dealers you choose to connect with (name, phone, address)
  • Payment processors for transactions
  • Law enforcement when legally required

We do NOT sell your data to third parties for advertising.

4. Data Security

Your data is stored securely using Appwrite cloud infrastructure with encryption at rest and in transit. We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Delete your account and all associated data
  • Opt-out of promotional communications

6. Account Deletion

You can delete your account at any time from Settings → Delete Account in the app. This will permanently remove all your data within 30 days.

7. Children's Privacy

Scrapify is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect data from children.
